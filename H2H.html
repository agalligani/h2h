And thanks for coming.

This portal will serve as the window into
our world for the next 8 months. Over
this period, Wendy and I will plan and
experience one of the greatest and
most challenging adventures of our
lives. With little more than survival
instincts, travel calluses, a large
motorcycle and a fist full of dollars, we
will spend 6 months winding our way
around the circumference of the globe,
documenting in these pages our best
and worst days.

Please read on and check back often
to understand the WTF of the journey, to
see where on Earth we are currently
holing up, and to relive our pain and
glory through words and fotos as we
endure hostile border guards,
enterprising customs officials, and the
odd highway bandit. See you on the
road!
